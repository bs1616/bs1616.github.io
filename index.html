Homework Planner

This is an app meant to help with time management, which combats procrastination. This helps reduce stress because after you have done your assignments, you will not have to stress over them. It changes your "flight" or "freeze" response to a "fight" response, which has you get your work done. Use this every day after school to plan the rest of your day.
